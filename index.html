Privacy Policy for Huey

Effective Date: August 1, 2025

Huey is a Chrome extension designed to help creatives organize and manage color palettes within their browser. Your privacy is important to us.

What Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device and never sent to any server.

Permissions

Any permissions requested by the extension are solely for enabling functionality like saving colors or copying to clipboard. No data is collected or shared.

Third Parties

Huey does not use any third-party services, analytics, or trackers.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. All changes will be posted on this page.
